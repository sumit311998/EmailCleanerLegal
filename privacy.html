Privacy Policy: Gmail Cleaner – Sovereign Edition

Last Updated: February 2026 [cite: 2]

1. Introduction

Gmail Cleaner – Sovereign Edition is committed to protecting your privacy[cite: 4]. This document explains how we handle your data when you use the Extension to manage your Gmail inbox[cite: 5].

2. Information We Access

The Extension requests access via the https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.modify scope[cite: 7, 8]. This allows the extension to search emails for marketing keywords, apply labels, and move unwanted threads to your Gmail Trash[cite: 8].

3. Limited Use Disclosure

Our use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Service User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. We do not sell, trade, or transfer your Gmail data to third parties[cite: 21].

4. Data Storage and Transmission

  • Sovereign Architecture: All processing occurs locally within your Chrome browser and your personal Google Apps Script (GAS) environment[cite: 15].
  • No Third-Party Servers: Your email data is never transmitted to us or any third party[cite: 17].
  • Retention: No Gmail data is stored by the Extension[cite: 18]. Authentication tokens are stored locally and cleared upon logout[cite: 19].
